Open Enterprise Search Platform (OpenESP)

Welcome to OpenESP, the Open Source Enterprise Search Platform. The project develops an Apache 2.0 licensed distro of Apache Solr. With "distro", we mean Solr as the core plus other components which are frequently needed in an enterprise search setting.

We believe the next logical step in the Lucene/Solr "revolution" is to bring to the masses a free, open distro which enables enterprises around the globe to be up and running with searching their databases, file systems, ECMs and other repositories within minutes after downloading OpenESP. For that reason we include Tomcat and ManifoldCF out of the box.
